Hello! I am Mehul Tikekar.

I am a Ph.D. candidate in Electrical Engineering and Computer Science at the Massachusetts Institute of Technology. I work on low power circuit design for video compression and decompression in the Digital Integrated Circuits and Systems Group headed by Prof. Anantha Chandrakasan.

I received a B.Tech degree at the Indian Institute of Technology Bombay in 2010 and an S.M degree at MIT in 2012.
