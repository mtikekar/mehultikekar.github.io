Hello! I am Mehul Tikekar.

I study Electrical Engineering at the Massachusetts Institute of Technology. I work on low power circuit design for video compression and decompression in the Digital Integrated Circuits and Systems group headed by Prof. Anantha Chandrakasan.

I received a B.Tech degree at the Indian Institute of Technology Bombay in 2010 and an S.M degree in 2012 at MIT, where I am currently a Ph.D. candidate.
