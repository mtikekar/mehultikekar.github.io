Hello! I am Mehul Tikekar.

I am an Accelerator Architect at Waymo. Previously, I worked at Analog Devices in the Analog Garage as a research scientist and engineer developing algorithms, software and hardware related to signal processing, machine learning and computer vision.

I received my Ph.D. in Electrical Engineering and Computer Science from the Massachusetts Institute of Technology in the field of low power circuit design for video decompression. At MIT, I was co-advised by Prof. Anantha Chandrakasan and Prof. Vivienne Sze. I received a B.Tech degree at the Indian Institute of Technology Bombay in 2010 and an S.M degree at MIT in 2012.
